C major seventh arpeggios

The following diagrams show all C major seventh chord tones together in one diagram, and then again, separated into positions appropriate for use as arpeggios aligned with the “CAGED” framework.

This also serves as an example of a general approach to the generation of position subsets. These diagrams are not the results of passing seventhChord to positionScale. Instead, majorScale is passed to positionScale and the output of that is further processed by filterScaleDegree to eliminate all but degrees 1, 3, 5, and 7. Thus the output is guaranteed to be in alignment with the 5 complete major scale positions, which is not a guarantee that positionScale alone can make.
